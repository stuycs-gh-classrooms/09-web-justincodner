Why Do Triangles Matter?

The study of triangles is an ancient art practiced even by ancient civilizations. Why study triangles some of you may ask. Well, the study of triangles becomes the corner stone for the understanding of space and form. The study of triangles is also necessary in other fields of science such as physics, engineering, and navigation. On top of that, logical thinking and problem solving are skills that come with the study of triangles
